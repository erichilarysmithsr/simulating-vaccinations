In this folder you find various networks for the simulation.

All the networks called "barabasi"... are created by
network_generator.create_barabasi_in_file(). Look there for additional
information. The filename is:
"barabasi_(number of vertices)_(number of new edges every new vertice adds)"

project90.tsv (5493 vertices):
The project90.tsv is a network created by the Office of Population Research
of Princeton University. For further information look here:
https://opr.princeton.edu/archive/p90/
